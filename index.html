Precaution To take while surfing the web

Stay updated

Keep software, operating systems, and security applications up-to-date to patch vulnerabilities and protect against cyber threats.

Use strong, unique passwords

Create strong passwords and consider using a password manager to securely store them. Enable multi-factor authentication whenever possible.

Be cautious with emails

Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown or unsolicited emails. Be wary of phishing attempts that try to trick users into revealing personal information.

Secure your devices

Use reputable antivirus and anti-malware software on all devices, including computers, smartphones, and tablets.

Be cautious on social media

Adjust privacy settings and limit the amount of personal information shared on social media platforms. Be mindful of friend requests or messages from unknown individuals.

Use secure connections

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. If necessary, use a VPN to encrypt internet connections and protect sensitive data.

Educate yourself

Stay informed about common cyber threats and scams. Be aware of the latest techniques used by cybercriminals to target individuals.

Verify sources

Backup data regularly

Regularly backup important files and documents to an external hard drive or a secure cloud storage service to prevent data loss in case of cyber attacks.

Secure financial transactions

Use secure and reputable payment methods for online purchases. Avoid entering financial information on unsecured or suspicious websites.

Monitor accounts

Regularly monitor bank statements, credit card bills, and other financial accounts for any unauthorized transactions or suspicious activities.

Be skeptical

Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true or high-pressure tactics used in online communications. Trust your instincts and verify information before taking any action.

Limit personal information sharing

Avoid oversharing personal details online, especially on public forums or social media platforms.
